Our Soapstone is carved by the artists of SMOLArt, a group of artisans in Tabaka, Kenya, the heart of soapstone crafts. Established in 1990, SMOLArt, a WFTO member, ensures fair wages and supports community projects for better living conditions, education, and health.

Soapstone is mined in Kiisi, Kenya, and carved into sculptures ranging from cream to black. Artisans smooth the stone with sandpaper and water, polishing it to a high gloss or painting it with vibrant African motifs.

All soapstone products are handmade using simple tools like screwdrivers and pangas, reflecting the artisans' skill and dedication.
